Short Summary

This bill would establish a Select Committee of the House consisting of six Republicans and six Democrats to investigate on gun violence and report on recommendations and resolutions, including legislative proposals that would increase safety and preventative measures against gun-related criminal activity. Investigations would include causes of mass shootings, methods to improve the background checks on firearm purchasers, closing loopholes that allow some domestic abusers continued access to firearms, and strengthening penalties for gun trafficking and illegal purchasing.
