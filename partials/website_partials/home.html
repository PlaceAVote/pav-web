Our Mission

PlaceAVote is committed to restoring real representation by sharing with representatives how their districts are voting on each legislative bill and issue, while encouraging them to vote in Congress based on the majority of their constituents.

What does Placeavote do?

Read

PlaceAVote shares bills in plain, straightforward language that makes them easy to understand and lets you focus on the actual points of the bills.

Vote

More convenient than standing in line at the polls and just as secure (if not more) than marking your ballot behind a curtain, your vote on PlaceAVote is 100% anonymous.

Debate

We're a safe and secure forum for not only voting, but also sharing and discussing issues that are important to you.
