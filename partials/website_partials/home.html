The Problem

Our representatives seem to represent their biggest donors, not us. Our voice is blocked by the money that gets politicians into power.

The Solution

Let's vote representatives into congress who want to listen and act on the voice of their voters. Let's use technology to prioritize the issues we care about and loudly communicate them to those we put in power as our delegates.

What does Placeavote do?

Anonymously Vote

We enable you to vote for or against any legislation put in front of congress, no one will know what stance you took on an issue, unless you want them to.

Publicly Discuss

Choose to voice your opinions on subject matters, or be safe in the knowledge of keeping total anonymity with your vote, like the good old days.
